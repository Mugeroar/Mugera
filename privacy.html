Information we collect

  • Email address & name: When you register, we collect your email address and the name you provide. This information is used only for account management, password resets, and essential service communications.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our site (pages visited, features used) to improve our services. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Payment information: All payments (whether subscription, contract, or one‑off) are processed by secure third-party gateways (e.g., M-Pesa, card processors). We do not store or have access to your payment details.
  • Contract / transaction data: For services involving contracts or written agreements (especially in automotive and real estate), we may collect additional information necessary to fulfill that agreement (e.g., identity verification for property transactions). Such collection will be disclosed in the specific agreement and handled with heightened security.
  • Sensitive information: We explicitly ask you not to provide any unnecessary sensitive personal data (such as health records, political opinions, or religious beliefs) via forms or account profiles. Where absolutely required (e.g., for legal compliance in real estate), we will collect it through secure, dedicated channels with explicit consent.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account, verify your identity (including enhanced verification for automotive/real estate transactions where required), and provide customer support.
  • To send you important updates about your subscription, contract, service changes, or security alerts. (You may also receive occasional inspirational content – you can opt out of non-essential emails.)
  • To facilitate partnership services: if you register for a program co‑offered with an accredited institution, we may share necessary data with that partner as disclosed at the time of registration.
  • To analyse and improve the performance and user experience of our website and services across all eight pillars.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our platform and prevent fraud or abuse, especially in high‑value pillars like real estate and automotive.

Sharing & disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share necessary information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or conducting our business – provided they agree to keep your information confidential.
  • For partnership programs (accredited courses, professional services), we will share only the information needed to deliver that specific service. The partner’s privacy policy will also apply, and we’ll highlight this during registration.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of I AM MUGERA, our users, or others.

Data retention & security

  • We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you services (including fulfilling contracts). If you close your account, we will delete your information within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for real estate may be kept longer due to regulatory obligations).
  • We implement strong security measures including encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect your information. For sensitive transactions (automotive sales, property purchases), we employ additional verification and secure document handling. We continuously work to safeguard your data and do everything we can to ensure customer protection.

Your rights & choices

  • You can access and update your account information (name, email) at any time through your profile settings.
  • You may request deletion of your account by contacting us. Note that some information may be retained as necessary for legal or operational reasons (e.g., past transaction records).
  • You can opt out of non-essential emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails.

Cookies & tracking

  • Our website uses minimal cookies (small text files) to remember your login status and theme preference. These are essential for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.
  • We may use anonymous analytics tools (like Firebase Analytics) to understand aggregate usage patterns – this data cannot be used to identify you.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with data without your consent, please contact us immediately so we can address the situation.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices (for example, new partnerships or pillars) or for legal reasons.
