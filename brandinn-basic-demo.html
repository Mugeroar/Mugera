Hero Slider
This is your brand's welcome mat. Add up to 5 high-quality images that showcase your best work, team, or products. Each slide can have custom text.

Services
List up to 5 services you offer. Each service gets an icon, title, description, and price in Kenyan Shillings. Perfect for showing exactly what you do.
