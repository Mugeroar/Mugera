Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about supporting our work

How will my contribution be used?

Your support directly funds content creation, community programs, event organization, and operational costs. Each payment reason corresponds to specific initiatives that drive our mission forward.

Will I receive a receipt for my payment?

Yes, we provide digital receipts for all payments. After you submit your payment details and we confirm receipt, we'll send a formal receipt to your email address within 24-48 hours.

What is the minimum amount I can contribute?

The minimum payment amount is KES 100. However, we recommend amounts starting from KES 500 to make a meaningful impact on our initiatives.

Can I make recurring payments?

Currently, we only accept one-time payments. However, you can support regularly by making multiple payments as you wish. We're working on implementing recurring payment options in the future.

How do I know my payment was received?

After submitting the form, you'll receive immediate on-screen confirmation. We then manually verify all payments and send a confirmation email within 24-48 hours. You can also contact us directly at info.iammugera@gmail.com for status updates.

What if I need a refund?

Refunds are available within 7 days of payment for valid reasons. Please contact us at info.iammugera@gmail.com with your payment details and reason for refund request. Note that refunds may incur transaction fees.
