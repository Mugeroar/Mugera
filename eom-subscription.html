Frequently Asked Questions

How do I renew my subscription?
Your subscription auto-renews monthly on your renewal date. To manually renew, click the "Renew Subscription" button above or visit our partnership page to make a payment of KES 100.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (Buy Goods Till: 4001100), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely. Use your email as the account number for M-Pesa payments.
When will I lose access after cancellation?
If you cancel, you'll retain full access until the end of your current billing period. After that, premium content will be locked, but free issues remain available.
How do I update my payment information?
Click "Manage Payment" above. For M-Pesa users, simply make a new payment to Till 4001100 with your email as the account number. For card payments, contact support for assistance.
What happens if my payment fails?
You'll receive a notification and have a 7-day grace period to update your payment method. After that, access to premium content will be temporarily suspended.
Can I upgrade to a different plan?
Currently we offer a single premium plan at KES 100/month. Future plans may include annual and lifetime options. Stay tuned for updates!
