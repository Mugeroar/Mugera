Frequently Asked Questions

How do I renew my subscription?
Your subscription auto-renews monthly. To manually renew, click the "Renew Subscription" button above or visit our partnership page.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (Buy Goods Till: 4001100), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely.
When will I lose access after cancellation?
If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. After that, premium content will be locked.
How do I update my payment information?
Click "Manage Payment" above. For M-Pesa users, simply make a new payment to the Buy Goods Till (4001100) with your email as the account number.
