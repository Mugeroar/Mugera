The Mazda CX-5 Experience

The Mazda CX-5 represents the perfect harmony of Japanese craftsmanship, innovative SkyActiv technology, and the award-winning KODO design language. Every curve, every surface, and every detail has been meticulously crafted to deliver an emotional driving experience that connects driver and machine.

Exterior Design

KODO: Soul of Motion design language brings the CX-5 to life, even when standing still. The powerful stance, flowing lines, and premium finishes create a presence that commands attention.

Signature Front Grille

The bold wing-shaped grille with chrome accents defines the CX-5's presence. LED headlights with adaptive front lighting system automatically adjust to road conditions.

Dynamic Side Profile

Sculpted body panels catch light and shadow, creating depth and motion. The rising beltline and muscular wheel arches house 19-inch alloy wheels.

Rear Design

Clean, powerful rear with dual exhaust outlets and LED combination lights. The tailgate design provides easy loading access to 875L of cargo space.

LED Lighting System

Full LED headlights with signature daytime running lights. The lighting design incorporates Mazda's signature wing element.

19-inch Alloy Wheels

Machined-finish alloy wheels with a sophisticated multi-spoke design. Fitted with 225/55R19 tyres for optimal grip and comfort.

Panoramic Roof

Power-sliding panoramic glass roof brings natural light into the cabin and enhances the feeling of spaciousness.

Interior Craftsmanship

Step inside to discover a sanctuary of premium materials, thoughtful ergonomics, and Japanese attention to detail. Every touchpoint has been designed to delight.

Driver-Centric Cockpit

All controls are oriented towards the driver for intuitive operation. The horizontal axis design creates a sense of stability and spaciousness.

Premium Leather Seating

Nappa leather upholstery with contrast stitching. Front seats feature 8-way power adjustment, heating, and ventilation. Ergonomic design reduces fatigue on long journeys.

Mazda Connect System

10.25-inch colour display with Mazda Connect infotainment. Includes Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, built-in navigation, and a 12-speaker Bose sound system.

Leather-Wrapped Steering

Heated leather steering wheel with mounted controls for audio, cruise control, and driver information display. Paddle shifters for manual control.

Spacious Rear Cabin

Generous rear legroom with reclining seatbacks. Rear air conditioning vents, USB charging ports, and a fold-down centre armrest with cupholders.

Versatile Cargo Space

875 litres of cargo space with the rear seats up. 60/40 split-folding rear seats create a flat load floor for bulky items. Power tailgate included.

Exterior Features

  • KODO Design Language

    The evolution of Mazda's design philosophy creates a sense of motion even at standstill. Sculpted surfaces play with light and shadow.

  • Adaptive LED Headlights

    Full LED lighting with automatic levelling and cornering function. The headlights pivot up to 15 degrees to illuminate curves.

  • Power Sliding Panoramic Roof

    Two-panel glass roof with power sunshade. Opens to let in fresh air and natural light, enhancing the sense of space.

  • 19-inch Alloy Wheels

    Exclusive machined-finish alloy wheels with dark metallic accents. Wrapped in high-performance tyres for optimal grip.

  • Power Folding Mirrors

    Auto-dimming, power-folding side mirrors with integrated LED turn signals and puddle lights.

  • Dual Exhaust

    Sporty dual exhaust outlets integrated into the rear bumper design, finished with chrome tips.

Interior Features

  • Nappa Leather Upholstery

    Premium leather seating surfaces with contrast stitching. Ventilated and heated front seats with memory function.

  • 10.25" Mazda Connect Display

    Widescreen display with intuitive rotary commander control. Features Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration.

  • Bose Premium Sound System

    12 speakers including subwoofer and centre channel. Bose TrueSpace technology creates an immersive audio experience.

  • 7-inch Digital Instrument Cluster

    Multi-information display with customizable information. Includes digital speedometer, fuel economy, and safety system status.

  • Tri-Zone Automatic Climate Control

    Individual temperature settings for driver, front passenger, and rear seats. Includes air filtration system.

  • Heated Steering Wheel

    Leather-wrapped steering wheel with heating function. Mounted controls for audio, cruise, and driver display.
