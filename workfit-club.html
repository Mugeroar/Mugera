Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WorkFit Club

Membership type

We offer a subscription-based model. After subscribing, you can book sessions prior to each day you need the service. Flexible and convenient!

Pricing

Very flexible pricing based on family size, number of sessions, and activities chosen. We'll work with you to find a plan that fits your budget.

Time

Sessions available in morning hours, afternoon, and evening up to 7pm. Choose the time that suits you best.

Meal plans

We provide custom meal plans based on individual or family preferences. Healthy, balanced, and tailored to your goals.

Other activities

We offer monthly outdoor activities (hikes, team games, adventures). They can be paid as part of the subscription or separately.
