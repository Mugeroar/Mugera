About Pro Accounts

Everything you need to know about becoming a service provider

What is a Pro Account?

A Pro Account unlocks the ability to offer services on our platform. Whether you're a delivery assistant, driver, coach, trainer, or tutor, the Pro Account gives you access to tools, earnings, and a verified status that builds trust with customers.

Key Benefits

  • Verified provider badge on your profile
  • Access to service-specific dashboards
  • Real-time earnings tracking in your wallet
  • Ability to accept and manage tasks
  • Priority customer support
  • Flexible work schedule
